Hospital Management System

Introduction

The Hospital Management System (HMS) aims to streamline hospital operations by digitizing patient records, doctor schedules, appointments, and billing. This system ensures efficiency, reducing manual errors and improving service quality.

Modules

  • Patient Management: Add, edit, delete, view patient records.
  • Doctor Management: Add and list doctor profiles, assign doctors to patients.
  • Appointment Management: Schedule, view, and cancel appointments.
  • Billing System: Generate and store medical bills.
